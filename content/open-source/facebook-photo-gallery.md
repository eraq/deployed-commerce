This plugin embeds all albums from a Facebook page into a WordPress page via a shortcode. Add a photo or album to your Facebook page and it is automatically reflected on your WordPress site, no manual updates needed.

Most similar plugins require you to specify album IDs to embed individual albums. That means updating your site every time you add a new album. This plugin queries your Facebook page directly and pulls in everything automatically.

It also works with lightbox plugins to add a lightbox effect to Facebook images while keeping users on your site.
